Why Taranga
What the price actually buys

A home here isn't priced like the apartment next door — because it isn't one.

The lake in front can never be built over. Only four to six families share your floor. Eight in ten homes are corners — light on two sides, and a foyer that opens to no one else. Ceilings rise to 10.35 feet; floor-to-ceiling glass hands you the water. None of this can be added later, at any price. That is what your home secures here — not square feet, but a position on this lake that simply cannot be built again.
